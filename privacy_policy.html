1. Information We Collect

We gather various types of information to provide and improve our services to you. This information may be collected automatically as you interact with our Site or provided voluntarily by you.

Personal Data

This includes any information that can directly or indirectly identify you. We collect personal data that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Register for an account on our Site.
  • Submit inquiries or requests through contact forms.
  • Engage in online chats or participate in message boards.
  • Provide feedback or interact with our customer support.

Examples of personal data collected may include your name, shipping address, email address, phone number, age, gender, and interests.

Derivative Data

Our servers automatically collect certain information when you access the Site. This "derivative data" helps us ensure the security and operational efficiency of our services. It includes:

  • Your Internet Protocol (IP) address.
  • Browser type and version.
  • Operating system.
  • Access times and duration of visits.
  • Referring and exit pages.
  • Pages viewed on our Site.

This data is primarily used for analytical purposes to enhance your browsing experience and for internal reporting.

Financial Data

If you engage in transactions on our Site, we may collect limited financial information related to your payment method (e.g., credit card type, expiration date). However, please note that for security, sensitive financial details like full credit card numbers are generally not stored directly by us. Instead, they are handled by secure third-party payment processors. We strongly recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these processors.

2. How We Use Your Information

Accurate information allows us to provide a seamless, efficient, and personalized experience. We use the information we collect for various purposes, including to:

  • **Manage Your Account:** Facilitate account creation and management, including authentication and user preferences.
  • **Process Transactions:** Handle your purchases, orders, and send you confirmations, invoices, and updates.
  • **Enable Communication:** Support user-to-user interactions and facilitate communication between you and our service team.
  • **Personalize Experience:** Develop a customized profile to tailor your experience on our Site, offering relevant content and recommendations.
  • **Improve Site Operations:** Monitor usage patterns, analyze trends, and troubleshoot issues to enhance the efficiency and functionality of the Site.
  • **Provide Updates:** Inform you about important changes, updates, or new features related to our Site and services.
  • **Offer New Services:** Introduce you to new products, services, promotions, or special offers that may be of interest.
  • **Conduct Business Activities:** Perform other necessary business functions, such as data analysis, auditing, and preventing fraud.

3. Disclosure of Your Information

We may share your information in specific circumstances as described below. We prioritize your privacy and will only disclose information when necessary or legally required.

By Law or to Protect Rights

We may disclose your information if legally required to do so, or if we believe such action is necessary to:

  • Comply with a legal obligation (e.g., court order, subpoena).
  • Protect and defend the rights or property of Rcon Finishing Units.
  • Prevent or investigate possible wrongdoing in connection with the Service.
  • Protect the personal safety of users of the Service or the public.
  • Protect against legal liability.

Third-Party Service Providers

We may engage third-party companies and individuals to facilitate our Service ("Service Providers"), to provide the Service on our behalf, to perform Service-related services, or to assist us in analyzing how our Service is used. These third parties have access to your Personal Data only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

Examples of services they may provide include payment processing, data analysis, email delivery, hosting, customer service, and marketing support.

Marketing Communications

With your explicit consent, or where legally permissible and with an opportunity for you to opt-out, we may share your information with third parties for marketing purposes. This includes delivering targeted advertisements or promotional content. You always have the right to withdraw your consent or opt-out of such communications.

4. Security of Your Information

We are committed to safeguarding your personal information using robust administrative, technical, and physical security measures. These measures are designed to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction of your data.

While we implement industry-standard security protocols, it is important to acknowledge that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure. Therefore, while we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. We continuously review and update our security practices to protect your information as effectively as possible.
